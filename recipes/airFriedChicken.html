Air Fried Chicken

Air Fired Chicken

Ingredients

  • Favoirte Spice Blend
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt / Pepper
  • Chicken Breast
  • Parsley

Preperation Steps

  1. heat air fryer to 380 degrees F
  2. coat chicken evenly with your spice blend
  3. apply thin layer of olive oil over the surface of the breast
  4. place chicken in heated air-fryer and cooked for 15 mins
  5. let chicken rest for 3 mins and enjoy!
