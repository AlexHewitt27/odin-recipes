Air Fired Tofu

Ingredients

  • tofu
  • Olive Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Seeds

Preperation Steps

  1. heat air fryer to 350 degrees F
  2. pour the soy sauce over the tofu and let soak for 10 mins
  3. place tofu in the heated air fryer for 20 mins
  4. garnish with sesame seeds and enjoy!
